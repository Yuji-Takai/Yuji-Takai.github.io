Hi, I'm Yuji!

France, England, Japan, United States, and Russia; those are the countries Yuji lived in. He is a frequent traveler, software engineer, and novice SCUBA diver. After finishing his undergraduate degree in Computer Science at Georgia Institute of Technology in May 2020, he will start working at Bloomberg as a software engineer from September 2020.

Recent Projects

GIT LIT ATS

NASA Undergraduate Student Launch Initiative (USLI) is an annual collegiate rocket competition held by NASA. Yuji is currently working on the Apogee Targeting System (ATS) of GIT LIT (Georgia Institute of Technology Launch Initiative Team).

Shogi AI Project

This is an upcoming project that Yuji is looking forward to work on. He is thinking of making the AI learn through matches of professional shogi players.

FlyAware

For his junior capstone project, Yuji worked on a Chrome Extension, FlyAware, that calculates the carbon footprint of your flight when purchasing tickets on Delta, American, United, and Southwest Airlines.
